# I have ADHD and I just finished a 20-minute article in 4 minutes

**Audience**: ADHD / Focus
**Format**: Genuine reaction / demo
**Duration**: 25 seconds
**Hook strength**: Very high (emotional, specific, identity-based)

## Script

**HOOK (0-2s)**
Text overlay: "I have ADHD and I just read a 20-minute article in 4 minutes without losing focus once"
Visual: Genuine surprised/emotional expression

**SETUP (2-6s)**
Text: "For context, I normally can't get past paragraph 2 without my brain checking out"
Visual: Show scrolling through a long article, eyes glazing over, scrolling back up

**REVEAL (6-10s)**
Text: "This app shows you one word at a time. Your brain literally can't wander."
Visual: Open Speed Read, show the word-by-word display

**PROOF (10-20s)**
Text: "Watch my focus — I can't look away even if I try"
Visual: Screen recording of RSVP in action. Words appearing one at a time. No distractions. Progress bar filling up. Show completion.

**CTA (20-25s)**
Text: "Speed Read RSVP — it's free to try. Link in bio."
Visual: App icon + heartfelt "this actually changed things for me"
