# How I went from a 2.8 to Dean's List in one semester

**Audience**: Students
**Format**: Storytime / Transformation
**Duration**: 25 seconds
**Hook strength**: Very high (transformation, specific numbers, curiosity gap)

## Script

**HOOK (0-2s)**
Text overlay: "How I went from a 2.8 GPA to Dean's List in one semester"
Visual: Before/after grades (blurred for "privacy")

**SETUP (2-6s)**
Text: "I wasn't studying harder. I was just actually doing the readings for the first time."
Visual: Stack of textbooks / reading lists

**EXPLAIN (6-10s)**
Text: "I found an app that lets me speed read any article. 300, 400, 500 words per minute."
Visual: Open Speed Read → share an article → show WPM selector

**PROOF (10-20s)**
Text: "What used to take me 3 hours of reading now takes 40 minutes. So I actually do it."
Visual: Screen recording of Speed Read in action. Quick montage of multiple articles being consumed.

**CTA (20-25s)**
Text: "Speed Read RSVP. The reading hack nobody talks about."
Visual: App icon + grades improving graphic
