# The CEO asked me how I always know what's going on

**Audience**: Professionals
**Format**: Storytime
**Duration**: 20 seconds
**Hook strength**: Very high (authority figure validation, career aspiration)

## Script

**HOOK (0-2s)**
Text overlay: "The CEO asked me how I always know what's going on in the industry"
Visual: Professional setting, subtle flex energy

**SETUP (2-5s)**
Text: "I'm not even in a senior role. But I reference articles from that morning in every meeting."
Visual: Meeting room / Slack messages

**REVEAL (5-9s)**
Text: "My secret: I speed read every relevant article before 9am"
Visual: Morning routine — phone, coffee, Speed Read open

**DEMO (9-16s)**
Text: "10 articles. 15 minutes. Every single morning."
Visual: Screen recording — rapid fire through articles in Speed Read. Show multiple articles being consumed at 450+ WPM.

**CTA (16-20s)**
Text: "Speed Read RSVP. Be the most prepared person in any room."
Visual: App icon + App Store link

## Caption
The CEO asked me how I always know what's going on in the industry. I'm not even senior. I just read faster than everyone else. #career #corporate #ceo #productivity #workhack #speedreading #9to5 #promotion

## Notes
- Career aspiration is a huge TikTok motivator
- "I'm not even senior" creates relatability + aspiration
- Key emotion: admiration → envy → "I need to do this"
