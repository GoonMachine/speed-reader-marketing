# My roommate thought I was cheating because I finished the reading list in an hour

**Audience**: Students
**Format**: Storytime
**Duration**: 22 seconds
**Hook strength**: Very high (conflict, disbelief, social proof)

## Script

**HOOK (0-2s)**
Text overlay: "My roommate accused me of cheating because I finished the entire reading list in an hour"
Visual: Shocked / defensive expression

**SETUP (2-5s)**
Text: "She literally said 'there's no way you read all of that, you're going to get us both in trouble'"
Visual: Dramatic reenactment or text conversation

**REVEAL (5-9s)**
Text: "So I showed her"
Visual: Pull up an article → Share → Speed Read RSVP

**PROOF (9-18s)**
Text: "Her face when she watched me read a 15-page paper in 3 minutes"
Visual: Screen recording of Speed Read at 450 WPM eating through an article. Show the pages flying by.

**CTA (18-22s)**
Text: "Now she uses it too. Speed Read RSVP - link in bio"
Visual: App icon
